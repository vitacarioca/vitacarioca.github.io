Suggestions

Christ the Redeemer and Sugar Loaf

Rio's two best attractions (including one of the Seven Wonders of the World), where you can see the beautiful city from different perspectives.

Selaron steps and Santa Teresa walking tour

Get to know Santa Teresa, one of the most characteristic neighborhoods of the city and the place where I live. Wonderful views, craft shops, restaurants, bars and the famous tram that connects the neighborhood to the center of Rio.

The beaches of Rio de Janeiro

Fully customizable tour based on how many beaches you want to visit and how long you prefer to stay on the beach. It is a tour that can be done by bicycle or by car.

Tijuca National Park by bike

A unique experience, an unforgettable day, which will allow you to experience the carioca forest in all its sounds and smells. Here toucans, monkeys and amazing views are waiting for you. It is a tour that requires a good level of training.

Niteroi

The cariocas say that the best thing about Niteroi is the privileged view of Rio, I think that's true, not because Niteroi is ugly (it`s beautiful), but because the views of the wonderful city are truly incredible.

Sunrise in magical places

Come and see the colors of the wonderful city when the sun rises, with the sugar loaf and Guanabara bay in the background.

Who am I

My name is Stefano Glauco Gorini. I am italian, born and raised in Milan and a few years ago I moved to my favorite city: Rio de Janeiro

I was lucky to travel and visit many places around the world. I have been to 30 countries, but Rio has always given me something more.

The forest inside the city, the amazing views, the sunsets, the beaches, the tropical weather, the dances, the music and the carioca soul have conquered me since the first day i arrived. Here I met my wife and began building my new life.

I started and finished the course to become an international tourist guide, specialized in Rio de Janeiro and natural attractions and i started doing what i like most: show the world why Rio has conquered me.

I want to make you fall in love with the place I fell in love, I want to make you come back home with a smile on your face and a more incredible experience to be able to tell.
